About _

Hello, my name is Oliver Siket. I live and work in Bristol, England.

I've been bouncing all around the world in an attempt to find my right fit. I lived in Japan for a little over 2 years, after finishing my studies there, I needed a new direction. A path which would allow to control my own career and future, so I just decided to move to Bristol and I took the first leap into coding.

Since then it has slowly been consuming more and more of my time as it developed into a newfound passion. I feel like it is the perfect environment where I can show my creative side.
I'm highly motivated, proactive, good team player who enjoys a challange, with a constant drive to grow both professionally and personally.

When I'm not coding or thinking about design, you can surely find me at car meets, taking photos, cycling with friends or playing games.
